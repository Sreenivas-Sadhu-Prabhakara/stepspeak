How it works

  1. 1

    Paste your steps

    Drop in a recipe, a manual, or any list. Each line becomes one step. Common list markers are cleaned up for you.

  2. 2

    Walk through, hands-free

    See one large step at a time. Move with the buttons or the arrow keys, and let the built-in voice read each step as you go.

  3. 3

    Tune it to you

    Pick a voice, slow it down, make the text huge, switch to dark. It all stays on your device for next time.

A reading aid, not a safety device. stepspeak reads back exactly what you paste and can mis-split unusual text. It is a general-purpose reading and accessibility aid—not for safety-critical or medical procedures. Always verify instructions against the original source and use at your own risk.
